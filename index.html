Features

Our Farewell Management System Provides a number of features for Students, Teachers, and Administrators.

Voting System

Students can vote for items to be selected for the final menu. Similarly they can vote for performances that are to be held in the farewell party. The best one is selected for the menu/performances.

Attendance Tracking

Administrators can track attendance of students, teachers, as well as any family members they may bring.

Task Allocation

Administrators/Organizers can assign tasks related to decorations, venue setup, and other responsibilities to student volunteers.

Budget Tracking

Administrators/Organizers can track budget and monitor expenses related to venue booking, catering, decorations, etc.
